With growing fear, Will became overwhelmed and many times he seemed to hear Fry's impatient footsteps--although he didn't know if this was his illusion or real.

伴随着渐渐增大的恐惧，Will变得手足无措起来，很多次他似乎听到了Fry急躁的脚步声——虽然他不知道这是他的幻觉还是真实。

He saw Tess running ahead at a very fast pace.

他看到Tess在前面以很快的速度跑着。

Yet Tess and Will were clearly not familiar with each other, and Will might have been aware of Tess's unfamiliarity with the surroundings; he might not be able to continue running after Tess into the unknown ......

然而Tess和Will显然并不互相熟悉，Will也许已经知道Tess对这里环境的不熟悉，他也许不能继续跟着Tess奔向未知……

The idea was that it was a junction, and although it was late at night, the crowds had not diminished significantly in such a market.

想到这里，正是一个路口，虽然已是深夜，但在这样的街市下，人流并没有明显减少。

There was a moment's hesitation as Will looked straight ahead at Tess.

Will望着Tess笔直向前的目光，出现了片刻的迟疑。

Once again, Will heard Fry's footsteps.

Will再一次听到Fry的脚步声了。

He was about to miss the junction and the narrower street was reflected in front of him.

即将错过这个路口，前面映出的是更加狭窄的街道。

The choice was a matter of thought.

选择就在一念之间。

Following the flow of people, he turned left along the road, no one would notice the hunched back in this dense crowd.

顺着人流，他沿着道路向左转弯，在这个密集的人流中，没有人会注意到这个佝偻的背影。

It was a desperate choice, for he too had never been to this place, except that he thought it less likely that he would be caught by his master after the separation.

这是孤注一掷的选择，因为他也从未去过这个地方，只是他认为分离之后被他主人抓到的可能性更小一些。

Tess was still running forward, not noticing the disappearance of the silhouette behind him.

Tess仍在向前跑动，没有注意到身后的身影的消失。

 

Tess and Will finally climbed to the top of the stairs, when a loud noise came from downstairs, the two turned their heads to see that Mr. Fry used his body to knock the locked door open.

Tess和Will终于爬到了楼梯顶端，这时楼下传来一阵巨大的响声，二人转头一看，原来是Mr.Fry用身体把被反锁的大门撞开了。

The door slab fell to the ground, splashing a mud.

门板倒在地上，溅起一阵泥泞。

Mr. Fry glared at them viciously, immediately catch up and also climbed the stairs.

Mr.fry恶狠狠地瞪了他们一眼，立马追上来也爬上了楼梯。

Will ran forward with all his strength, turned the corner and he hit something:

Will用尽全身力气向前跑去，转过街角，他撞上了什么东西：

"Mr. Fry! Don't ‘it me, I'm so wrong!"

“Mr. Fry！别打我了，我错了！”

"Will! Open your eyes, it's Harriet!"

“Will！睁开眼，我是Harriet啊！”

Will looked up and Harriet was looking at him with wide eyes and a worried face: "Where's Tess? Isn't she with you?"

Will抬起头，Harriet正睁大眼睛看着他，满脸担忧：“Tess呢？她没有与你一起么？”

Will stammered and replied, "We escaped together... ... No...? Where did ‘e go? I lost ’er!"

Will支支吾吾的回答：“我们一起逃出来的... ...啊？她去哪里了？我跟丢她了！”

Harriet lowered her head and sighed: "Tess has probably fallen into Fry's hands. We'd better get going and find them. Don't worry, I bet Fry won't make a move on me."

Harriet低下头，叹了一口气：“Tess很可能已经落入Fry的手里了。我们最好赶紧出发找到他们。放心，我打赌Fry不会对我动手。”

Will wanted to say something else, but Harriet pulled him out into the street.

Will还想说些什么，但Harriet把他拉到了街上。

Through the crowd of people, Harriet saw a familiar silhouette. She squinted her eyes and identified it as Tess, and she waved at her in a hurry—

穿过拥挤的人流，Harriet看到了一个熟悉的轮廓。她眯起眼睛，确定了那就是Tess，她连忙向她挥了挥手——

After a long walk, I turned back once again to make sure Fry was no longer following me. I suddenly had a twinge of concern about Will's whereabouts.

走了很久，我再一次回头确认Fry已不再跟随着我。我突然对Will的下落有了一丝担忧。

Can no longer wait. Thinking of this, I turned around and headed off as fast as I could.

已经不能再等了。想到这里，我急转方向，以最快的速度掉头行进。

I don't know how long it took, I finally saw the familiar street. At the intersection, I turned left to investigate Will's trail.

不知多长时间之后，我终于看到那条熟悉的街道了。在那个十字路口上，我向左转弯，探寻Will的踪迹。

The road soon came to an end, where there was nothing but a "No Trespassing" sign standing there.

路很快到了尽头，在哪里，除了立在那里的一块“禁止通行”的标牌之外，没有任何东西。

I turned around again and ran to the other way. There was the downtown area, the crowd of people holding my body hostage, making me miserable.

我又转身向路的那一侧跑去。那里是闹市区，拥挤的人流挟着我的身体，使我痛苦不堪。

Suddenly, I saw a familiar figure. Was that Harriet? Wiping down the sweat-soaked eyes, I confirmed this suspicion. I could faintly see Will's thin body following behind her. However, just as I was about to join Harriet, another familiar figure appeared from the other side.

突然，我看到了一个熟悉的身影。那是Harriet么？擦下被汗水浸润的眼眶，我确定了这个猜测。隐约能看到Will瘦弱的身躯跟在她的背后。然而正要与Harriet会合时，另一个熟悉的身影从一旁出现。

It was the tall Fry, whom I would never forget, coming the other way and glancing just a little to the right to see Harriet and Will.

这是我永不会忘记的，高大的Fry。他从另一条路赶过来，只要稍一向右瞥，就能看到Harriet和Will。

I couldn't stand the fact that this was happening. So I shouted as loud as I could to distract Fry. He did turn his gaze to this place, and then met me with its fierce gaze.

我不能忍受这一切的发生。于是我竭尽全力喊了一声，来分散Fry的注意力。他果然将目光转向这里，然后用它凶恶的目光与我互相对视。

Once again, I turned and ran backwards. It was dimly lit and Fry could not see if Will was following me and managed to get confused by me and chase in the direction I was running.

我再一次转身向后跑去。那里灯光昏暗，Fry并不能看清Will是否跟在我身边，成功地被我迷惑，向我跑的方向追赶。

But this is where I felt a pang in my chest, maybe I had been running too long.

不过这是我感到一阵胸闷，也许是跑得太久了。

It didn't matter, let's fight for as long as we can.

没有关系，能争取到多久就算多久吧。

I dodged toward the side of the road and fell to the ground.

我向路边一闪，趴在了地上。

Looking at Fry's faraway, I instantly understood Tess's action. However, in any case, it was no longer possible to care about her now.

望着Fry的远去，我瞬间理解了Tess的行为。不过，无论如何，现在已经顾不上她了。

I brought Will to the front door of the house and gently opened the door. There was no light in the whole building, only darkness.

我将Will带到家里的大门前，轻轻开门。整个楼没有一点灯光，只有一片黑色。

Father must have already gone to sleep. So, I'll explain it all to him tomorrow.

父亲应该已经睡去了吧。那么，明天再向他解释这一切吧。

I brought Will upstairs, he was already coughing from running too long. I kept him as far away from his parents' bedroom as possible, and eventually brought him to the kitchen by the fireplace.

我将Will带上楼，他已经因为跑了太久在不断地咳嗽。我尽可能让他远离父母的卧室，最终把他带到了厨房的壁炉前。

It was now the latter part of the night and sleepy surrounds Will as he quickly drifted off to sleep.

现在是后半夜了，困倦环绕着Will，他很快睡过去了。

Finally, it was time to look for Tess. Really tired though.

终于可以去寻找Tess了。虽然真的很累。

I opened the door once more, left, locked it gently again, and ran in the direction Tess had escaped.

我再一次打开门，离开，再将门轻轻锁紧，然后沿着Tess逃跑的方向奔去。

The scene in front of me kept switching between sleepiness and reality.

眼前的景物在困倦和现实中间不断切换。

I was sure that success was within my reach.

我确信成功已在我能够触及到的地方了。
